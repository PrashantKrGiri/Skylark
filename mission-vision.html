Founder & Chairman

Since the very inception, Skylark has been an organization that is based on honesty, integrity and realism. The organization is backed by not only our strong and experienced team but also our professionalism, relentless commitment and operational excellence. Our endeavour is to provide innovative, comprehensive customized cost effective Services to our clients in India & Africa.

Satisfaction of our esteemed clients is of paramount importance to us backed by a strong backbone defined by our best qualities and management practices. We endeavour to build a strong and lasting relationship with our clients, employees and associates.

Mission

Our mission is to strive towards achieving excellence by providing committed, efficient and cost effective solutions, while constantly adding value through innovation in our services and up- gradation of our skills. Our ultimate goal is to establish Skylark Cagers India Pvt Ltd as the leading Services provider Company in India & Africa and build Skylark Group as a Company that will play a key role in Urban & Economic Growth of India & Africa.
