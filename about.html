About Us

Since our inception in 2000, Skylark Cagers Security Pvt Ltd has emerged as a global leader in integrated security services, committed to delivering unmatched solutions worldwide. Boasting a workforce of over 13,000 professionals and a robust network spanning 32 branches across India, we stand as a beacon of excellence.

Our expertise lies in Secure Solutions, Facilities Management, and cutting-edge Security Systems. Skylark Cagers Security goes beyond, offering comprehensive services including Training, Event Security, Security Consultancy & Risk Management, Guarding, 24/7 Monitoring, Investigation & Quick Response Teams, and a state-of-the-art control room.

Serving a prestigious clientele across diverse sectors, we take pride in delivering excellence in security, training, and beyond.
