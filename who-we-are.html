Who We are

Established in 2000, skylark cagers security pvt ltd stands as a global leader in integrated security services, dedicated to providing unparalleled security solutions worldwide. With a workforce exceeding 13,000 professionals and an extensive network spanning 32 branches and area offices across india, our commitment to excellence is unwavering.

Our core competencies include secure solutions, facilities management, and cutting-edge security systems. Beyond these, skylark cagers security offers a diverse range of services encompassing training, event security, security consultancy & risk management, guarding, 24/7 monitoring, investigation & quick response teams, and a state-of-the-art control room.

Proudly serving an esteemed clientele across various sectors, skylark cagers security takes pride in delivering top-notch services in security, training, and more.
