He is ‘the’ man behind the success of Skylark Group. Dr. Rao has a long list of achievements and accolades along with various significant recognitions from Govt. of India. With a strong focus on Business Development & Project Implementation he intends to take Skylark to Great Heights. He is a team player and believes in developing Leaders and Achievers in the company.

An effective leader and motivator he is always full of ideas and solutions.

His leadership abilities and expertise in the area of construction have been instrumental in the growth and success of our company.
